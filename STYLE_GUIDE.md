# StyleMate Design System

## Brand Personality

StyleMate should feel:

* Elegant
* Fashion-forward
* Premium
* Warm
* Personalized
* Feminine without being overly feminine
* Modern luxury
* Editorial rather than corporate

Avoid:

* Bright neon colors
* Generic blue SaaS themes
* Heavy dark mode defaults
* Overly playful UI
* Cluttered dashboards

Think:

* Aritzia
* Reformation
* Pinterest
* Sephora
* Glossier
* Vogue
* Luxury shopping concierge

# AI Assistant Design

The AI assistant should feel like:

```txt
Your personal stylist
```

Not:

```txt
A chatbot
```

Examples:

Good:

* "Perfect for a summer internship."
* "This pairs well with neutral accessories."
* "A versatile piece you'll wear repeatedly."

Bad:

* "Processing request..."
* "Generating recommendation..."
* Robotic responses
